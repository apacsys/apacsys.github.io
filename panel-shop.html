Panel Design & Fabrication

A-PAC Controls offering customized solutions to meet your specific needs. We work closely with you to understand your requirements and design, fabricate and deliver a control panel that meets your expectations and specifications.

At APAC Controls, our group of professional engineers, designers and wiremans will provide you with the full scope of panel building services. If you dont have your own electrical design we will do it for you. We will design, select components, procure, assemble, wire componets and perform internal testing of the control panels for your project ranging from simple control station to multi-panel control system. With our internal testing procedure we will ensure that all electrical and control componts is fully functional, all electrical wiring and labeling complete according to the design.

On top of the panel design and fabrication we also offer a range of services to support your project needs, including electrical and mechanical installation, on-site startup and commissioning, maintenance and technical support.
